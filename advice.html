What Level Of Profesionalism Are You Trying To Achieve?

Buisness Formal

Men's Attire

  • Dark colored suit and tie preferably blue or grey. Avoid bright colors or busy patterns.
  • Button Down shirt preferably white or light blue
  • Belt (tuck shirt)
  • professional, closed toed shoe like an oxford or loafer

    • Women's Attire

  • Tidy Dresses, skirts or slacks
  • Shirts should either be a button down or a blouse with a blazer
  • Minimal accesorization
  • professional, closed toed shoe like oxford or loafer

    • Smart Casual

    Men's Attire

  • sports jackets, ties, khakis, button-down shirts, polos, dress shoes, boots, clean sneakers and belts.

    • Women's Attire

  • blazers, dresses, sweaters, trousers, skirts, blouses, heels, flats, jewelry and scarves.

    • Buisness Casual

    Men's Attire

  • trousers, slacks, khakis, button-downs, polos, or sport coats.(Jackets are optional but can be used to accessorize)
  • shoes include loafers, lifestyle sneakers (with leather or canvas), oxfords or boots.

    • Women's Attire

  • pencil skirts, blouses, button-down shirts, trousers, khakis, blazers and sweaters.
  • Accessorize with simple jewelry and belts
  • Shoes include flats, loafers, mules, boots or heels

    • Casual

    Men's Attire

  • T-shirts, button-down shirts or sweaters on top
  • Bottoms might include jeans, khaki pants or shorts
  • Casual shoes can include sneakers, loafers or sandals.

    • Women's Attire

  • T-shirts, blouses and sweaters on top
  • Bottoms might include jeans, cropped pants or shorts
  • Casual shoes can include sneakers, loafers or sandals
